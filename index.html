Kin — Moments Together, for iPhone

Kin helps couples and families create shared phone-light Moments — quiet the apps that pull you away, and be present with the people in front of you.

Common questions

How do I connect with my person?

In Settings, invite them by their iCloud email or share your invite link. When they accept, your phones connect through iCloud — Kin has no accounts to create.

Why does Kin ask for Screen Time permission?

Screen Time (Apple's Family Controls) is how Kin quiets apps during a Moment. Apple's design keeps your app choices and usage invisible to Kin and to your partner.

What if I need my phone during a Moment?

Ask your person for a pause — they'll get the request instantly. For urgent situations, the emergency pause always works immediately, no approval needed.

Can my partner see what I do on my phone?

No. Kin shares only Moment events: started, completed, pause requests. Never app names, usage, or content. Presence, not surveillance.

How do I disconnect from a partner?

Settings → Disconnect. The link is removed on both sides. Either person can disconnect at any time.
